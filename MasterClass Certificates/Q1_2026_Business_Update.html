Key Insights
06
What's Working — and Where to Push Harder
61%
AI is the Revenue Engine
AI Courses account for 61% of total Q1 revenue ($797K) with a 1.7× ROAS — the strongest volume-efficiency combination in the portfolio. The Feb 16 spike (9.2× ROAS) signals untapped burst potential during the right market conditions.
7.5×
NVIDIA & Microsoft Signal Scale Upside
NVIDIA delivers 7.5× ROAS and Microsoft 6.3× — both far above portfolio average — but on small spend budgets ($10K and $9K respectively). Scaling spend on these two products could unlock meaningful revenue with high efficiency. GTC and the Microsoft Rewards program are immediate levers.
1.0×
NSF & Moonshots Need Efficiency Work
Together NSF and Moonshots represent $376K in revenue (29% of total) but run at 1.0–1.1× ROAS, barely breaking even on ad spend. Creative refresh and tighter audience targeting are needed before scaling spend on these products further.
$650K
Production Economics Are Locked In
The cost-per-course target of $650K is achieved — a 30% reduction from prior baseline. With production economics improving, every additional course added to the catalog comes online with better unit economics, making the path to $8M more capital-efficient.
